This extension just displays the existing New York Times Connections board and waits for the
point where three rows have been identified.  It then clicks on the remaining four tiles
and submits them to the game.

The extension doesn't transmit any information other than what a
user's press of the tiles or the 'Submit' button would transmit.

The extension doesn't require any kind of registration to be used. It doesn't care who uses it.

No Personal Identifying Information (PII) is collected at any time.

Of course, any user of this extension assumes the usual liability.

Anyone involved with the production of this extension has no
affiliation (or if you don't mind... connection) with the New York Times or its Games
section.
